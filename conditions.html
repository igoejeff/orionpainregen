Chronic pain, joint degeneration, and systemic inflammation — we treat the root cause, not just the symptoms.

Condition 01

Chronic Pain

You don't have to live with it.

Chronic pain affects over 50 million Americans, and conventional medicine typically addresses it with a cycle of painkillers, anti-inflammatories, and cortisone injections — all of which manage symptoms without treating the underlying cause. Worse, many of these approaches accelerate tissue degradation over time.

Regenerative medicine breaks that cycle by directing healing to the actual damaged tissue — whether it's a fraying tendon, a deteriorating joint, or chronically inflamed connective tissue.

Common Symptoms

  • Persistent joint or muscle aching lasting 3+ months
  • Pain that worsens with activity or weather changes
  • Limited range of motion and flexibility
  • Fatigue and poor sleep from constant discomfort
  • Dependence on pain medications that stop working

Condition 02

Osteoarthritis

Cartilage can regenerate. Surgery isn't inevitable.

Osteoarthritis is the most common joint condition in the world — affecting 32.5 million Americans — and it is one of the most undertreated. The standard of care often ends at pain management until the patient is "bad enough" for joint replacement surgery.

Regenerative medicine offers something conventional orthopedics cannot: a genuine path to cartilage regeneration, inflammation reduction, and structural stabilization — all without going under the knife.

Common Symptoms

  • Joint stiffness, especially in the morning
  • Pain that worsens with activity and improves with rest
  • Swelling and tenderness around affected joints
  • Grinding, popping, or clicking sensations
  • Reduced flexibility and range of motion

Condition 03

Chronic Inflammation

The root of most chronic disease — and it's treatable.

Chronic systemic inflammation is now recognized as the underlying driver of most major chronic diseases — from joint degeneration to cardiovascular disease to cognitive decline. Yet most treatments continue to suppress it superficially rather than resolve it at the source.

Through functional medicine principles and targeted regenerative protocols, Dr. Davenport works to identify the causes of your inflammatory burden — and address it at the cellular level.

Common Symptoms

  • Chronic joint swelling, redness, and warmth
  • Persistent fatigue and low energy
  • Brain fog and poor cognitive clarity
  • Recurrent flare-ups with no clear trigger
  • Systemic aches that feel diffuse and hard to localize
