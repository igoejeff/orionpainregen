Exosome Therapy

Exosomes are nano-sized signaling vesicles released by stem cells — the biological messengers that tell your body exactly how and where to heal. They carry growth factors, genetic instructions, and anti-inflammatory signals directly into damaged cells, triggering regeneration at a level no drug can replicate.

  • Billions of signaling exosomes per dose
  • Growth factors & mRNA cargo
  • Powerful anti-inflammatory cytokines
  • No cells — zero rejection risk
