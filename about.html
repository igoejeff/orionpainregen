Dr. Davenport grew up in Spokane, Washington, and earned his bachelor's degree in biology from Brigham Young University. He attended medical school at Midwestern University in Glendale, Arizona — where he and his wife fell in love with the Phoenix area — and completed his anesthesia residency at Ohio University in Columbus, serving as Chief Resident.

As Chief Resident, Dr. Davenport recognized that pain management — not anesthesia — was where he could change lives most profoundly. He went on to complete an interventional pain fellowship in the greater Phoenix area, performing thousands of procedures and developing a deep understanding of which therapies are genuinely helpful and cost-effective for patients.

After holding positions across small private practices and large corporate models, Dr. Davenport founded Orion Pain — a practice built on the most technologically advanced procedural options available. He has invested in cutting-edge machines that treat damaged tissue including torn tendons and arthritic joints using non-invasive methods that allow the body to heal itself.

"My goal is to help reduce pain and increase function in all my patients — without the crutch of addicting narcotics."
